KaunTech

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

KaunTech ("we", "our", or "us") is a business card scanning app that helps you capture, organise, and enrich professional contacts. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

Business card data

Names, job titles, companies, phone numbers, email addresses, LinkedIn profiles, and any other text present on scanned cards.

Camera & images

Camera frames are processed on-device for OCR. Images are not stored on our servers unless you explicitly save a contact.

NFC & QR data

Data read from NFC tags or QR codes is processed locally and stored only if you choose to save the contact.

Account information

Your Google account email and name when you sign in via Google OAuth. We do not store your Google password.

Usage & subscription data

Token usage, subscription tier, and trial status to manage your plan.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To parse and save business card contacts to your account
  • To run AI enrichment (company intel, lead scoring) on saved contacts via OpenRouter
  • To sync contacts to your device's native address book (only if you grant permission)
  • To manage your subscription and token balance
  • To send transactional emails relevant to your account

3. Third-Party Services

We share data with the following services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — database and authentication hosting (EU/US data centers)
  • Google OAuth — sign-in only; we request no sensitive Google scopes beyond your basic profile
  • OpenRouter / AI providers — contact text is sent to AI models for OCR refinement and enrichment; no data is used to train third-party models under our agreements

4. Data Retention

Your contacts are stored as long as your account is active. You can delete individual contacts or your entire account at any time from the app settings. On account deletion, all your data is permanently removed within 30 days.

5. Security

All data in transit is encrypted via TLS. Data at rest is encrypted by Supabase's managed Postgres infrastructure. Row-level security (RLS) ensures you can only access your own contacts.

6. Children's Privacy

KaunTech is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We will notify you via the app or email for material changes. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
